Request Account Deletion

If you would like to delete your Vytal Healthcare account and associated personal data, you may request account deletion by contacting us using the email address below.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to vytal.healthcare1@gmail.com.
  2. Use the subject line Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include the email address associated with your Vytal account.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your account.
  • Your username.
  • Your email address.
  • Your profile information.
  • Your medical information stored by Vytal.
  • Your uploaded documents and prescriptions.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained where required by applicable laws, regulations, or for legitimate security and fraud prevention purposes.

Processing Time

We aim to process verified account deletion requests within 30 days.
